GNU Coreutils is a collection of essential command-line programs that form the basic utilities of most GNU/Linux systems.
These are the standard versions of tools like ls, cp, mv, grep, cat, and many more.

GNU Coreutils is divided into three major groups:

1. File Utilities (file and directory operations)

3. Shell Utilities (miscellaneous everyday commands)
