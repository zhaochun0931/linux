bzip2 --version


Compression by bzip2 is better than compression by gzip , but executing it takes longer.

The file name is given the extension .bz2




# compress

bzip2 file.txt


This will compress file.txt and produce file.txt.bz2









# decompress

bunzip2 file.txt.bz2

This will decompress file.txt.bz2 and restore it to file.txt
